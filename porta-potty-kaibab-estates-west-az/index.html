Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the average cost to rent a porta potty nationwide?

Standard units average $85–$150/month. Luxury trailers range from $800–$2,500/weekend. Prices vary by location, duration, and service frequency.

How often should portable toilets be serviced on a job site?

Weekly is standard for 10 people per unit. In hot climates like Texas or Arizona, or high-traffic events in Florida, twice-weekly is recommended.

Do you offer ADA-compliant units for weddings?

Yes. All event packages in California, around Georgia, and nationwide include ADA options, clearly marked and placed near main access points.

Can I rent just for one day?

Yes. We offer 1 day porta potty rental for inspections, pop-up events, or emergencies, no long-term commitment required.

How far in advance should I book for a wedding?

For luxury restroom rentals, book 4–8 weeks ahead, especially during peak season in states like South Carolina or North Carolina.
